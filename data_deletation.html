mAIster Account and Data Deletion

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your mAIster account and associated data in compliance with our privacy policy and applicable data protection laws.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please email us at help.maister.app@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Your registered Google account email.
  • Details to verify your identity (if needed).

We will acknowledge your request within 3-5 business days and complete the deletion process within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving a deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • User account information (email and profile data).
  • History data (previous problems and solutions).

Data Retention Period

In certain cases, we may retain data for up to 30 days after account deletion due to legal, security, or operational requirements (e.g., fraud detection, legal compliance). This data will be securely deleted once the retention period expires.

For more information, see our Privacy Policy.
