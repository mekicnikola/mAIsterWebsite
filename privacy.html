1. Introduction

Welcome to mAIster, your personal AI-powered assistant for home maintenance and problem-solving. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Account Information

  • Google Account Registration: We only collect your email address when you register using your Google account. No other personal information is gathered.

2.2. Usage Information

  • App Interactions: We collect data on app usage, including coin balance and problems submitted for AI assistance.
  • Problem History: We save user-submitted problems and corresponding AI-generated responses as part of the app’s history feature, enabling users to review past issues and solutions.
  • Transaction Data: Purchases of coins through the Google Play Store are processed securely by Google.

2.3. AdMob and Advertising

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device Information: Device type and operating system.
  • Ad Interactions: Ad clicks and views.
  • Location Information: If location permissions are granted.

For more information on how AdMob handles your data, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage app features such as AI problem-solving assistance.
  • To allow users to access and review past problems and solutions.
  • To manage user accounts and display relevant ads.
  • To process in-app purchases securely through Google Play.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information, except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted providers like Google for authentication, payments, and ads.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to legal requests.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as required by law.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and breaches.

7. Your Rights
